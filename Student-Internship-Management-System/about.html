About SIMS

The Student Internship Management System is a centralized platform built to bridge the gap between students, companies and colleges — making internship discovery, hiring and progress tracking effortless for everyone involved.

🎯

Our Mission

To simplify and digitize the entire internship lifecycle — from discovery and application to selection and completion — through one unified, easy-to-use platform.

🌟

Our Vision

To become the go-to internship management platform for colleges and companies across India, empowering students to build meaningful early careers.

Who We Serve

👨‍🎓

Students

Discover relevant internships, apply in a few clicks, and track every application from one dashboard.

🏢

Companies

Post openings, review applicants, shortlist candidates and schedule interviews — all in one place.

🛡

Administrators

Verify accounts, moderate postings and generate platform-wide placement reports with ease.
